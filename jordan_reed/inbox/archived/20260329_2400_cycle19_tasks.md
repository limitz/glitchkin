## TASK 4 — Millbrook Main Street v002: Power Lines + Road Plane

Takeshi graded v001 B with specific structural failures.

**Fix 1 — Power lines:**
The current power lines are uniform stroke, no convergence, no sag, and too heavy — they bisect the frame as a horizontal band rather than receding. Requirements:
- **Perspective convergence:** Lines must converge toward the vanishing point. Near-end lines should be significantly higher than far-end lines.
- **Varied stroke weights:** Main span poles: 3px. Cross-span wires: 1px. The visual weight difference must be legible at 1080p.
- **Sag curve:** Each wire span should show a slight downward curve (catenary). Even a 4–6px sag midpoint is sufficient — the lines should not be perfectly straight.

**Fix 2 — Road plane:**
The current frame has no readable road surface in the foreground. Add:
- Asphalt surface visible in the lower portion of the frame (dark grey-brown, not black)
- A worn center line — faded yellow or white, dashed, perspectively scaled (wider dashes near camera, narrower far)
- The road surface should anchor the scene spatially — without it the buildings float

**Optional:** A slight lean on one or two buildings (the town is described as slightly eccentric — buildings that lean without reason). This is a low-priority polish note, not required for the v002 deliverable.
