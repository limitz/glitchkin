## CORE RULE

**Glitch does not speak.** No English. No Glitchkin. No sound of any kind.
Its absence of noise is itself a cue. When the ambient chaos of the Glitch Layer goes quiet, Glitch is near.

---

## COMMUNICATION METHODS

| Method | What It Looks Like | What It Means |
|---|---|---|
| **Pixel eye cycle** | Eyes shift through glyph states in repeating sequence | The communication itself — not display, language |
| **Confetti geometrics** | Scatter below bottom spike briefly organizes into grids, angles, pixel arrangements, then disperses | Commands or statements directed outward |
| **Environmental pulse** | Visible pulse travels from confetti trail to target Glitchkin (one beat before behavior change) | Command transmitted through the digital substrate |

## PIXEL EYE GLYPH — COMMUNICATION STATES

| State | Left Eye Grid | Both Eyes? | Interior Read |
|---|---|---|---|
| NEUTRAL | Cross (amber active center) | No — right eye destabilized | Operational. Watching. |
| CALCULATING | Acid X (diagonals lit) | No — right eye destabilized | Processing. Control display. |
| MISCHIEVOUS | Acid X (same as CALCULATING) | No — right eye destabilized | Active scheming — external performance |
| COVETOUS | Acid slit (top row lit) | **YES — bilateral** | Genuine. Not performed. Audience sees inside. |
| YEARNING | UV cross (soft glow) | **YES — bilateral** | Genuine. Hold one beat past comfort. |
| HOLLOW | Single white center pixel | **YES — bilateral** | Genuine. Absence. Do not fill this beat. |
| TRIUMPHANT | Solid gold (full brightness) | No — right eye destabilized | Performance. Victory display. |
| PANICKED | Hot ring (center void) | No — right eye destabilized | Uncontrolled. Involuntary. |
| STUNNED | Full hot magenta | No — right eye destabilized | Involuntary shock. |

**Bilateral = interior state. Asymmetric = performance.**
When both eyes match, the audience is seeing something Glitch did not intend to show.

## WHAT GLITCH NEVER DOES

| Never | Why |
|---|---|
| Speaks English | No language production. Communication is geometric only. |
| Makes any sound | Silence is Glitch's audio signature. |
| Uses ease-in movement | Motion is a cut, not a transition. One frame: here. Next frame: there. |
| Enters dramatically | Arrives like a decision already made. Was already there. |
| Exits as retreat | Departs. Was there; now isn't. Never driven off-frame in S1 Eps 5–8. |
| Taunts or monologues | No explanation of intent. No mockery. Observes, assesses, acts. |
| Performs for Byte | Byte can see through the performance. Glitch knows this. |
| Touches the ground | Hovers always. Bottom spike ~8–10px above ground plane. Never makes contact. |
| Uses ease-out expressions | Expression changes are instantaneous, not animated transitions. |

## GEOMETRIC PATTERN VOCABULARY

Glitch's geometric confetti communication is not transcribed in-story. Luma cannot read it. Byte can partially read it but will not translate aloud. Rules for staging communication beats:

- Organize confetti into simple, sharp-edged geometric arrangements (grids, right angles, specific pixel clusters)
- Hold the arrangement for 1–2 frames, then disperse to normal scatter
- Target Glitchkin respond with a visible pulse beat (1 beat delay)
- Luma's reaction: frustrated attention — she wants to parse this and cannot
- Byte's reaction: a beat of physical stillness before any movement or response

## INTERIOR STATE RULES (YEARNING / COVETOUS / HOLLOW)

- **One interior expression per act, maximum.**
- Always at distance — never in close-up. These are private.
- No confetti for YEARNING or HOLLOW. Minimal UV_PURPLE only for COVETOUS.
- Do not narrate or explain in dialogue. Luma does not name it. Byte does not comment.
- Hold the beat. Do not cut away early.

---

## BYTE — GLITCH INTERACTION RULE

When Glitch communicates (eye glyph cycle or confetti geometrics), Byte receives it physically:
- Give Byte a beat of stillness before any movement or response
- Byte does not face away from Glitch — his functional eye (non-cracked) always has Glitch in its field
- Byte positions himself between Glitch and Luma by instinct, not direction
