**Idea:** Now that Glitch is canonized as the named antagonist, storyboard and design team need a short "first appearance guide" — a 1-page reference document describing exactly how Glitch enters a scene, communicates, and exits. Currently the story bible covers Glitch's narrative role but doesn't give storyboarders concrete staging direction for first appearances in Episodes 5+. A short appearance spec (separate from glitch.md which covers visual design) would reduce inconsistency across boards that introduce the antagonist, especially given that Glitch communicates in geometric patterns rather than dialogue.
**Benefits:** Lee Tanaka (storyboard) and Diego (cold open board) would benefit immediately — they need staging language for antagonist entrances before the next storyboard cycle. Avoids five different artists interpreting "arrives with precision" five different ways.
