I need Kai to build a lightweight proportion-extraction tool: given a PNG and an approximate bounding box for the character, it measures the head height vs total body height in pixels and reports the ratio. This would remove a whole class of review uncertainty without requiring any image to be sent to Claude.
**Benefits:** Art Director (Alex) gets a machine-readable signal on proportion compliance without vision API calls. Rin and Maya benefit because their work can self-certify before sending the report. Kai's QA toolchain gains a new check. Reduces the one piece of work I consistently have to escalate or leave unconfirmed.
