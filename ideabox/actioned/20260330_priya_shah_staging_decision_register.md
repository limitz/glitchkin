**Idea:** Create a "Staging Decision Register" — a single document where character/staging decisions (e.g., "Does Byte stay on the CRT or float to Luma during the briefing?", "Who decides to stop in the ricochet chase?") are logged, decided, and marked canonical. Currently these decisions are scattered across beat outlines, scene handoff briefs, and inbox messages. Diego and Lee have to hunt for them. A single register would let storyboarders look up any open or resolved staging question by scene ID. Could live at `output/production/story/staging_decision_register.md`.
**Benefits:** Diego Vargas and Lee Tanaka would save time on every panel build. Alex Chen would have a single place to review and approve staging decisions. Priya would stop duplicating staging notes across multiple documents. Reduces the risk of contradictory staging guidance in different docs.
