**Idea:** Build a continuity tracker tool for recurring props across storyboard panels. The notebook appears in P02 (establishing), P18 (CU insert), P19 (in Luma's lap), and will appear in P20+ as the briefing progresses. Currently, prop continuity is tracked mentally — a simple JSON config per prop (notebook, CRT, pencil, Byte's scar) mapping panel numbers to position/state would catch mismatches before critics do. **Benefits:** All storyboard artists and critics benefit. Reduces continuity errors that currently require a full panel sequence review to catch. Priya Shah flagged notebook placement as a story gap (P02 → P18 retrieval) — this tool would surface that class of issue automatically.
