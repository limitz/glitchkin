**Idea:** Build a "hero expression isolator" tool that renders a single expression from any character sheet at 2x panel size (800×800px instead of the standard 373px panel). When critics say an expression "doesn't land," the problem may be that it's being evaluated at thumbnail scale surrounded by competing panels. A standalone large-render tool would let critics and the team evaluate the anchor expression (THE NOTICING, etc.) at a size where face detail, eye asymmetry, and hand gesture are all legible — giving more actionable critique data rather than mixed thumbnail/gestalt impressions.
**Benefits:** Helps critics give precise feedback on anchor expressions instead of sheet-level gestalt reactions. Helps Maya isolate whether an expression issue is design (fixable) or scale/context (can't be seen at current panel resolution). Would also be useful for pitch materials — a single large-format character moment image for the deck.
