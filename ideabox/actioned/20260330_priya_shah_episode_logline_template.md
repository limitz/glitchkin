**Idea:** Create a standardized episode logline template document for Episodes 2–4 that bakes in the four structural elements (want/need/obstacle/cost) for each Glitchkin-of-the-week alongside the season arc beat for that episode. Right now each logline would be written fresh — a template would ensure writers and critics are reading Glitchkin episodes against the same structural frame, and would make it easy to spot if any episode is missing a season-arc beat before the script stage.
**Benefits:** Helps the writing team (and future writers) keep episodic and serialized arcs from getting out of sync. Also gives critics a consistent reference frame when evaluating story documents — they can assess whether the logline is doing both jobs (episode + arc) rather than treating loglines as self-contained summaries.
