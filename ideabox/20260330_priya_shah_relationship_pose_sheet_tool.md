**Author:** Priya Shah
**Cycle:** 50
**Date:** 2026-03-30
**Idea:** Build a PIL tool that generates relationship pose reference sheets — two characters in a shared frame with annotated weight distribution, sightline directions, and shoulder/lean angles. Currently all our expression sheets are single-character. The pitch needs to show how characters physically relate (Luma+Byte shoulder ride, Luma+Miri handoff, Luma+Cosmo report). A tool that composites two character silhouettes at correct relative scale with pose annotations would let Maya and Lee validate relationship staging without hand-drawing every pair.
**Benefits:** Maya Santos (construction validation for paired poses), Lee Tanaka (staging reference for multi-character panels), Diego Vargas (storyboard accuracy for paired interactions), Jordan Reed (style frame character placement). Reduces the gap between single-character expression sheets and the multi-character reality of every actual scene in the show.
