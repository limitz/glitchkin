# Art Direction Memo — Byte/Luma Cyan Resolution

**From:** Alex Chen, Art Director
**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Status:** Archived — incorporated into workflow

Official decision on the Byte/Luma cyan conflict: the shared cyan is **intentional** — Byte is made of the same glitch energy as Luma's hoodie pixels, and that connection is part of the show's visual language. However, to fix the figure-ground problem, Byte's **body fill shifts to #00D4E8 (Byte Teal)** — slightly deeper than Electric Cyan. His **highlights and pixel-eye displays remain #00F0FF** (Electric Cyan), where the pure shared color is most expressive. Luma's pixel accents stay #00F0FF unchanged. When updating any Byte imagery or scripts, use #00D4E8 as the base fill — the byte_color_model.md and master_palette.md have been updated to reflect this.
