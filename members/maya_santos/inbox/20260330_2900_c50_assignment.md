## Assignment: Redesign Luma from the Ground Up

Our characters look like assembled geometry. The reference shows (Hilda, Owl House, Kipo) use similar simplicity but achieve life through curves, gesture, and proportion. Study the 20 images in `reference/shows/` for principles — keep our designs completely original.

### Deliverables:

1. **Luma Character Redesign Spec** (`output/production/luma_redesign_spec_c50.md`)
   - New proportions: 4.5 HU body height, eye size 32% of head radius
   - Gesture line definitions for all 6 expression poses
   - Control point maps for each body part (torso, arms, legs, head, hair)
   - Hair redesign: asymmetric, volumetric, breaks the head circle silhouette
   - Hand shape definitions: rest, fist, open, point/hold
   - Clothing details: hoodie collar, hem, cuffs, 1-2 wrinkle lines at elbows and waist

2. **Eye Shape Library for Luma** (`output/production/luma_eye_shapes_c50.md`)
   - Upper lid curve + lower lid curve definitions for each expression
   - Iris/pupil/highlight placement per expression
   - Must read at 200px thumbnail scale

3. **Silhouette Test Sheet** — black silhouettes of all 6 Luma expressions. Each must be distinguishable.

### Key Principles (from audit):
- **Curves not rectangles** — every outline is a bezier path, not a straight line
- **Gesture line in every pose** — the body is built around a curved spine
- **Taper everything** — shoulder>waist, hip>ankle, shoulder>wrist
- **Large expressive eyes** — 32% of head radius, with distinct lid shapes per expression
- **Hair with volume** — overlaps head boundary, asymmetric, recognizable alone
