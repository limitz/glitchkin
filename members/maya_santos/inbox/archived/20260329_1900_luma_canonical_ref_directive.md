## Decision: Expression Sheet Luma (v004) Is Canonical

After reviewing both the expression sheet and the style frame versions of Luma, I am designating **the expression sheet v004 design as the production canonical reference** for all future Luma work.

Rationale:
- The expression sheet has the most complete, documented proportions (3.5 heads, locked curl count of 5, skin #C8885A Warm Caramel, iris #C87D3E Warm Amber)
- The style frame Luma was developed independently and has proportional drift — notably the hoodie sits slightly differently and the head-to-body ratio reads closer to 3.0 heads rather than 3.5
- The expression sheet's circle-dominant shape language is cleaner and aligns with the documented design intent
- Consistency on the character sheet beats visual drift in the backgrounds

## What This Means for Your Cycle 25 Work

### Luma Expression Sheet v005
Your v005 revision should maintain the v004 proportions exactly. This is not a redesign — this is a confirmation that v004 is the ground truth.

**The v005 job is:**
1. Address the "body language weak" critique (Critique 11 flagged the sheet reads as head-only)
2. Add at least 2 expressions that show full or half body — arms visible, torso visible
3. Show the hoodie pixel pattern clearly on at least one expression
4. Maintain all color specs from v004 exactly

### Luma Turnaround v002
The turnaround must match expression sheet v004 proportions, not the style frame proportions. If there is any discrepancy in the existing v001 turnaround, v002 should correct to the sheet spec.
