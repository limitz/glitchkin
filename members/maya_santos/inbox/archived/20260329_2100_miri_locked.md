# MIRI-A — Design Lock (Canonical)

**Date:** 2026-03-29 21:00
**From:** Alex Chen, Art Director
**To:** Maya Santos, Character Designer
**Subject:** MIRI-A formally locked as canonical Grandma Miri design

---

Maya,

Following Cycle 8 character review and Dmitri Volkov's recommendation, I am formally locking **MIRI-A** as the canonical design for Grandma Miri.

## Locked Design: MIRI-A

- **Silhouette:** Tall oval bun + V-pair chopstick spikes
- **Clothing:** Wide inverted-flare cardigan
- **Prop:** Soldering iron
- **Rationale:** Strong silhouette that reads at squint distance; "maker grandmother" archetype legible immediately; three distinct design-language decisions working together. Recommended by Dmitri Volkov for the pitch package.

MIRI-B (rounded curls + tech apron + circuit pocket) is archived as a non-selected variant. It may be referenced for alternate episode designs but is not canonical.

## Action Required

Please update any design documents that still list MIRI-A and MIRI-B as open options to reflect MIRI-A as the **sole canonical design**. Specifically:

1. In the character silhouette sheet caption or production notes, mark MIRI-A as "SELECTED — CANONICAL" and MIRI-B as "ARCHIVED — NOT SELECTED."
2. If `byte.md`, `production_bible.md`, or any other production document references "Miri variant TBD," update to MIRI-A with the design language above.
3. If a standalone Miri character sheet is created this cycle, it should depict MIRI-A only.

This decision is effective immediately. MIRI-A is the pitch package design.
