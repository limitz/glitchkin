## DECISION: Glitch is the Corruption's avatar — a named Glitchkin consumed by the Corruption

**Full decision:**

"Glitch" is a named Glitchkin who has been entirely consumed and reorganized by the Corruption. Glitch is not the Corruption itself — the Corruption is abstract, systemic, a force. Glitch is its visible face, its instrument, its spokesperson in the narrative. What happened to Glitch is what the Corruption does to everything it touches: takes something alive and chaotic and replaces it with something precise and dead.

This serves the story in three ways:
1. **It makes the threat personal.** Byte knows who Glitch is. They have history (to be developed). The Corruption becomes real for the audience through this relationship, not through abstract description.
2. **It creates a visual contrast with Byte.** Two Glitchkin who went different directions after the Corruption appeared: Byte resisted and was exiled. Glitch surrendered and was elevated. Their conflict is the show's moral argument.
3. **It keeps the Corruption mysterious.** We never see the Corruption directly in Season 1. We only see what it has done to Glitch.

**Glitch's narrative role:**
- Named recurring antagonist
- Appears in episodes where the Corruption's influence reaches a peak
- Communicates in geometric patterns / pixel-perfect commands (the opposite of Byte's gruff English)
- Season 1 arc: Glitch tries to use Luma's CRT breach as a permanent Corruption portal. This is what Byte has been running from.
- Season 1 finale: The confrontation between Byte and Glitch is the emotional climax. Byte can only close the breach because he knows — from the inside — what Glitch is and what the Corruption offers.

**What does NOT change:**
- The visual design remains exactly as in glitch.md — diamond body, CORRUPT_AMBER palette, geometric spikes
- Glitch is NOT a "villain who wants to conquer the world" — that framing is too broad. Glitch simply executes the Corruption's directive: complete the reorganization of the Glitch Layer
- Generic Glitchkin (triangles, jagged polygons, chaotic energy) are not the same species as Glitch — they're just... messy. Glitch is what they become when the Corruption wins
