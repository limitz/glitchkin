## Core Characters
- **Luma** — 12yo, circles shape language. Fast improv voice. Want: adventure/proof. Need: patience.
- **Byte** — 6", oval body (NOT triangles — chamfered box retired Cycle 8). Clipped fragmentary speech. Want: self-sufficiency. Need: belonging without surrendering identity.
- **Cosmo** — 12yo, rectangle shape language. Glasses 7° tilt (never level). Warm precise voice. Want: understanding. Need: trusting instinct.
- **Miri (Glitch Layer)** — Glitchkin elder, warm anchor in cold world. Amber tones anomalous in Glitch Layer.
- **Grandma Miri** — Rounded square shapes. Knows more than she says. SHARES NAME WITH GLITCH LAYER MIRI — intentional story seed, season 1 finale.

## Tone
Comedy-adventure. Core: loneliness of perceiving differently, vindication through discovery. Warm/electric/reckless/specific/earned. NOT cynical/gross/frantic/vague/safe.
