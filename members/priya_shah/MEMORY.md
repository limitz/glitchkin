## Project: Luma & the Glitchkin
Comedy-adventure cartoon. 12-year-old Luma discovers mischievous pixel creatures (Glitchkin) living in her grandma's old CRT television. Three worlds: Real World (warm, domestic), Glitch World (electric, chaotic), Other Side (cool, mysterious, unknown).

## Core Characters (as I understand them)
- **Luma** — 12yo, curious, reckless, hoodie. The discovery protagonist.
- **Byte** — lead Glitchkin, loyal, small, electric teal. Luma's first friend in the Glitch world.
- **Cosmo** — confident Glitchkin, glasses, tends to be right. Comic foil?
- **Miri** — warm, welcoming Glitchkin. Grandmother archetype in Glitch world.
- **Grandma** — Luma's grandmother. Owns the CRT. Not a joke character.

## Tone
Comedy-adventure. The Glitchkin are mischievous — not evil. The emotional core is the relationship between Luma and Grandma, mediated by the Glitchkin who live in Grandma's TV.
