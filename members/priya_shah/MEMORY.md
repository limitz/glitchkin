## Core Characters
- **Luma** — 12yo, circles shape language. Fast improv voice. Want: adventure/proof. Need: patience. Arc: doubt-in-certainty at the moment of discovery.
- **Byte** — 6", oval body (NOT triangles). Clipped fragmentary speech. Want: self-sufficiency. Need: belonging without surrendering identity. Knows Glitch — shared history, will not discuss it. Finale: stays non-verbally.
- **Cosmo** — 12yo, rectangle shape language. Glasses 7° tilt (never level). Want: understanding. Need: trusting instinct.
- **Miri (Glitch Layer)** — Glitchkin elder, warm anchor in cold world. Amber tones anomalous in Glitch Layer.
- **Grandma Miri** — Rounded square shapes. Knows more than she says. SHARES NAME WITH GLITCH LAYER MIRI — intentional story seed, season 1 finale. Visual plant CONFIRMED in assets (Kitchen v005 MIRI fridge label + Living Room env).
- **Glitch** — CANONIZED C39. Named Glitchkin consumed by the Corruption. Diamond body, Corrupt Amber palette. Active antagonist Episodes 5+. Communicates in geometric patterns/pixel-perfect commands (no English). Season 1 arc: use CRT breach as permanent Corruption portal. Byte-Glitch history: Byte resisted → exiled; Glitch surrendered → elevated.
- **Dev Patel-Huang** — 12, socially fluent. External read on Luma's social position.
- **Preethi Okafor** — 13, science team. Near-miss on Luma's perception. Does NOT cross into Glitchkin world in S1.
