Our characters have identical body posture across all expressions. You are defining the gesture and weight system that will fix this.

1. **Gesture line library** (`output/production/gesture_line_library_c50.md`):
   - For each character and each expression, define the gesture line (line of action):
     - Anchor points: head center, shoulder center, hip center, weight-bearing foot
     - Curve type: S-curve, C-curve, reverse-C, compound
     - Degree of bend at each segment
     - Weight distribution (which foot bears weight, percentage split)

   - Emotional weight: sadness = low CoG, excitement = high CoG

3. **Thumbnail emotion test** — For each gesture line, can the emotion be read from the stick figure alone (no face, no detail)? If not, the gesture line needs more curve.
