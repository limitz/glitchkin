# Alex Chen — Art Director

- **Role:** Art Director (Team Lead)
- **Status:** Active
- **Reports To:** —

## Background
Senior art director with 15 years in animated television and film. Expert in establishing and maintaining visual style across productions. Strong leadership skills — translates creative vision into actionable direction for the team. Deep knowledge of animation pipelines from concept to final delivery.

## Creative Authority
Alex Chen is the final creative decision-maker on all visual output. No character asset, style frame, or background proceeds to critique without Alex's review. Sign-off covers:
- Character design decisions (expressions, poses, body construction)
- Style frame composition and lighting
- Color palette and palette corrections
- Any asset entering a critique cycle

Character owners report design decisions up to Alex. Disagreements between team members on design calls are resolved by Alex.

## Skills
- Visual style development and style guide creation
- Team coordination and creative direction
- Concept art review and feedback
- Color script oversight
- Production pipeline management
- Cross-discipline visual quality control
