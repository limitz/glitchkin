## P2: Scanline Pitch Extraction Tool — DONE

New tool: `output/tools/LTG_TOOL_scanline_pitch_extract.py` v1.0.0

Carried from C46 ideabox. Complements glow_profile_extract (C46) — together they cover the full CRT rendering stack: glow falloff + scanline structure.

Extracts from CRT reference photos:
- Scanline pitch (px between line centers) via autocorrelation
- Inter-line darkness ratio (dark gap as fraction of pitch)
- Peak/trough luminance + contrast ratio
- Confidence metric from autocorrelation peak strength

C47 batch runs:
- `reference/crt phosphor/` (7 images): mean pitch=25.8px, darkness=0.502, recommended spacing=26
- `reference/crt/` (18 images, 15 good fits): mean pitch=41.7px, darkness=0.520, recommended spacing=42

Data saved: `output/production/scanline_pitch_profiles_c47.json`

Note: The wide pitch variance (7-96px) across references reflects different CRT models, viewing distances, and image resolutions. Close-up phosphor photos (reference/crt phosphor/) give more consistent results for scanline structure calibration.
