### P1 — LTG_TOOL_expression_silhouette_v001.py (Ideabox Actioned)

Built and validated. Located at `output/tools/LTG_TOOL_expression_silhouette_v001.py`.

**Algorithm:**
- Per-pixel BG classification (corner sampling, tolerance=45) → binary silhouette
- Combined similarity metric: 0.6 × IoM + 0.4 × XOR_similarity
- FAIL ≥ 85%, WARN ≥ 72%
- Auto-detects grid from common sheet sizes; supports dark-BG sheets (Glitch)
- CLI: `python3 LTG_TOOL_expression_silhouette_v001.py sheet.png [--output sil.png] [--json]`
- Exit codes: 0=PASS, 1=WARN, 2=FAIL

**C33 Baseline results (all current expression sheets):**
| Sheet | Result | Worst Pair | Similarity |

| Cosmo v003 | FAIL | P1↔P2 | 95.9% |
| Byte v003 | FAIL | P7↔P8 | 88.8% |

*WISE↔KNOWING STILLNESS body silhouettes are intentionally identical — distinction is face-only per the narrative brief. This is documented in the tool output.
