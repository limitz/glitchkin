## Task 3 — DRW-18 Hair Warmth Documented (P2 — Priya)

**Files:** `output/color/palettes/master_palette.md` (DRW-18 entry), `output/color/style_frames/ltg_style_frame_color_story.md` (SF03 Key Color Tension section)

**Issue:** The color story text grouped "dark hair" with "warm values" in SF03. Priya correctly flagged that DRW-18 (`#1A0F0A`, 7% lightness) is visually not warm.

**Resolution:** The warmth is theoretical only. R:26 > G:15 > B:10 gives a hue of ~18°, but at 7% HSL lightness this is functionally imperceptible — the value reads as near-void dark, not warm.

**Changes made:**
1. master_palette.md DRW-18 entry: Added HSL lightness note (7%) and explicit "functionally imperceptible" warmth clarification. The entry now documents the decision clearly: DRW-18 does NOT contribute visual warmth to SF03; warm values are hoodie orange and skin only.
2. Color story doc SF03 "Key Color Tension": Removed "dark hair" from the list of "warm values." Clarified the distinction between hoodie orange + skin (visually warm) and hair (near-void presence). Noted the C32 correction inline for future readers.
