### 2. Glitch Expression Sheet v003 (Interior Desire)

**Problem addressed:** Nkechi Adeyemi C12 critique — "Glitch has six performance states but no accessible interior desire. Not yet a character — still a design."

**Action:** Grid expanded from 3×2 (6 expressions) to 3×3 (9 expressions). Three new interior desire states added:

| Expression | Read | Visual Signature |
|------------|------|-----------------|
| **YEARNING** | Watching what Glitch cannot name — and wanting it | Utterly still body; UV_PURPLE soft glow eyes (both); arms hanging low; no confetti; no mouth; deep indigo BG |
| **COVETOUS** | "I could take that. I should take that." | Lean tilt=+12; ACID_GREEN slit eyes (bilateral target lock); arms reaching; tight horizontal mouth; sparse UV_PURPLE confetti |
| **HOLLOW** | After the wanting, nothing came back | Deflated body (squash=0.88); empty bilateral stare (void+single white pixel); dangling arms; no confetti; single dim mouth dot; near-black cold blue BG |

Key design decision: YEARNING and HOLLOW use **bilateral eye states** (both eyes the same). All 6 performance states use destabilized right eye. The interior desire states break that pattern — when Glitch is truly feeling something, the asymmetry drops. Both eyes become the same. That is the tell.

**Canvas unchanged:** 1200×900 (≤1280px rule satisfied).
