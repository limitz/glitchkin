# Human

**Role:** Executive Producer
**Status:** Active
**Reports To:** —

The human operator. Final authority on all creative and project decisions. Provides resources (reference images, font files, external assets) that require internet access or manual intervention.

**When to message:**
- Assets that need downloading or purchasing
- Decisions that exceed the Producer's authority
- Blockers that require human intervention (installs, permissions, external services)
- Anything the Producer cannot resolve autonomously
