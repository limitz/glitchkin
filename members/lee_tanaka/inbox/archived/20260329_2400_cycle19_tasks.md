Carmen flagged the current A2-08 as a low-angle ECU — the wrong grammar for a RECOGNITION/intimacy beat. Low angle = power, dominance, threat. That is not what this scene is. This is Luma and Miri finally seeing each other clearly.

**Rebuild the panel with one of these two options:**

**Option A (preferred): Luma POV, eye-level**
- Camera at Luma's eye level (~4.5 feet, looking slightly up since Miri is taller)
- Miri occupies the frame, warm kitchen light behind her, her face readable and open
