## Summary of Persistent Issues (C15 to C46)

1. **Arms without shoulders.** Every human character across every asset type. Arms originate from body-edge points. No shoulder socket, no scapular mass, no deltoid curve. This makes every arm gesture read as a flag on a stick. This is the single most damaging structural flaw in the pitch.
2. **Symmetric bilateral stances.** Lineup, turnarounds, style frames -- characters default to even weight on both feet, arms mirrored. Physical life requires asymmetry. Weight on one foot. One shoulder higher. One hand doing something different from the other.
3. **Labeled poses vs. performed poses.** Characters are placed in position and annotated with their emotional state. The body does not perform the emotion. This is especially visible in SF06, P19, and the lineup.
4. **Luma's feet.** The duck-footed spec has never appeared in any rendered asset. Parallel feet in every view.
5. **Miri's motion spec** is the weakest motion sheet and needs a scale/proportion/weight pass.
